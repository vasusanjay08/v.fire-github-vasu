    Identity No:1=(x+y)^2=x^2+y^2+2xy

    Identity No:2=(x-y)^2=x^2-y^2+2xy

    Identity No:3= (x+y)(x-y)=x^2-y^2

    Identity No:4=(x+a)(x+b)=x^2+(a+b)x+ab

    Identity No:5=(x+y+z)^2=x^2+y^2+z^2+2xy+2yz+2zx

    Identity No:6=(x+y)^3=x+y^3+3xy

    Identity No:7=(x-y)^3=(x-y)^3=x^3-y^3-3xy-y

    Identity No:8=(x+y+z) (x^2+y^2+z^2-xy-yz-xz)=x^3+y^3+z^3-3xyz

    This are the 8 Algebraic Identities
